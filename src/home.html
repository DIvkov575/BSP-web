Python 101

Python 101 is an introductory course which covers everying you need to begin programming. Due to the dynamic pacing of our classes, course completion time varies

  • Fundamentals (5-10 classes): Variables, operators, conditions, Loops, builtin Data-structures
  • Intermediate (10-15 classes):OOP, Decorators, Lambda, Error hanadling, Dunder methods, FileIo
  • Advanced (10-15 classes): Student pursue's a topic of choice (Web-dev, Games, Machine-Learning, or Algorithms)

After a student graduates we provide a list of projects or 'next-steps', for students to pursue independently
