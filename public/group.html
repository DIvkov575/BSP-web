Python 101

This introductory python course covers everything you need to begin programming.
Due to the dynamic nature of our classes the time it takes to complete the full course may vary. No installation of any sofware is needed, just have a computer. Below are the different sections of the full python course. Click the 'Group' button for more info on our format.
You can even have your first class free on us.
Py101
(10-20) classes. Covers the fundamentals of python including (Variables, Operators, Conditions, Loops, Strings, common, lists, tuples, dicts, OOP)
Py102
(20-30) classes. Covers (OOP In-depth, Iterators, Recursion, Error Handling, generators, runtimes, data-structures, algorithms, Fileio, Git/Github)
PyGame
(20-30) classes. Creating games with the help of Python and Pygame lib Covers (Turtle/Canvas, Tkinter, Pygame, Prevailing-State's, Fileio/Executables)

Format

The reason we give a range for number of classes it takes to complete our course is because of our dynamic class format. Each student works to complete the material independently until they have a question or issue. This is when one of our tutors will step it to resolve any any/all student questions
